Fikir Amlak

Bio

FIKIR AMLAK has been professionally involved in the music industry for 20+ years as a singer/songwriter, multi-instrumentalist and producer. In the past 7 years, FIKIR AMLAK has seen his music released by various international labels in the form of 41 vinyl records, 17 CD albums and 41 digital singles. Since 2014, he has become a regular on the European ‘sound system’ tour circuit, frequently performing as a singer alongside King Alpha Sound System from the UK, as well as with the Barcelona-based reggae band, Go A Chant. Most recently Fikir Amlak collaborated with the jungle veteran Congo Natty on his first musical release in over 7 years to widespread acclaim. With several more vinyl releases, albums and digital singles slated for release in the coming years, along with having partnered with Georg Leitner Productions, a renowned Vienna-based artist booking agency and publishing house RoyNet based out of New York City, FIKIR AMLAK is poised to continue musical storytelling for years to come
