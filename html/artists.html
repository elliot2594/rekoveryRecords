Izachar Fikir Amlak
Izachar

Bio

Izachar is 23 years old and is a Producer/DJ and A&R on Congo Natty's (Rebel MC) new label - Rekovery Records. Coming from Hip-Hop roots, he started producing DnB in 2020, quickly catching the attention of Jungle legend - Congo Natty, who has been giving him guidance alongside the great Ray Keith. Izachar is currently working on a variety of projects to be released throughout the year, so expect some big 2021 movements on Rekovery Records.
